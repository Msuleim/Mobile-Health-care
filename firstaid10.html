Eye Injury or Puncture:

Step 1

The eye should be covered with a clean protective cover until the doctor is reached.

Step 2

Avoid pressing the eye cover or rinse the eye with water.

Step 3

Do not take aspirin, or any anti-inflammatory drug, it will increase bleeding.

Step 4

Do not take aspirin, or any anti-inflammatory drug, it will increase bleeding.

Step 5

You should go to the emergency, or the doctor immediately.
